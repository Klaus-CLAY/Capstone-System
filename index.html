ABOUT US

BUPC HEALTH SERVICES is a cornerstone of support and well-being within the campus community, committed to providing comprehensive healthcare services to students, faculty, and staff. Nestled within the vibrant academic environment of Bicol University Polangui Campus, the Health Service offers a spectrum of medical care, ranging from routine check-ups to specialized treatments. With a focus on preventive medicine, it endeavors to promote healthy lifestyles and empower individuals to take charge of their well-being through education and awareness initiatives. Additionally, the Health Service recognizes the importance of mental health and provides confidential counseling services to address the diverse emotional needs of students and staff.

Dedicated to accessibility and inclusivity, the Bicol University Polangui Campus Health Service ensures that healthcare remains accessible to all members of the campus community. Its convenient location and flexible appointment options make seeking medical assistance convenient for busy students and faculty members. Furthermore, the Health Service actively engages with the local community to foster collaborations and partnerships that enrich the health landscape of Polangui and its surrounding areas. As a trusted resource for health and wellness, the Bicol University Polangui Campus Health Service plays a vital role in nurturing a thriving and resilient campus community.

FEATURES

SIGN IN

Access your Health Services account securely to manage appointments, view medical records, and explore available resources. With the sign-in feature, individuals can stay informed and engaged in their healthcare journey, facilitating communication with healthcare providers and promoting holistic wellness.

BOOK

The booking feature ensures efficient utilization of time and resources, allowing students, faculty, and staff to prioritize their health conveniently.

REGISTER

Create your personalized profile within the Health Services system to streamline your healthcare experience. By registering, you gain access to tailored medical advice, appointment reminders, and a comprehensive overview of your health history, fostering a proactive approach to well-being.

FAQs

The Health Service offers a range of medical services, including consultations for general health concerns, first aid treatment, basic diagnostic tests, and referrals to specialists if needed. Additionally, it provides mental health support, health education programs, and emergency response services.

All students, faculty, and staff of Bicol University Polangui Campus are eligible to access the Health Service.

In case of a medical emergency, dial the emergency services hotline (e.g., 911) immediately. Additionally, notify the Health Service staff or any campus authority available for immediate assistance and coordination of medical response.
